COMMANDS LIST
===========================
virsh destroy myvm1
virsh undefine myvm1

===========================
VIRT-INSTALL COMMAND 
===========================
NOTE: Keep the DVD iso file in /tmp or elsewhere - not in '/root' - make its permission qemu:qemu

virt-install \ 
  --name myvm1 \ 
  --memory 2048 \ 
  --vcpus 2 \ 
  --disk size=20 \ 
  --cdrom /tmp/CentOS-7-x86_64-DVD-1810.iso \ 
  --os-type=linux \
  --os-variant rhel7
