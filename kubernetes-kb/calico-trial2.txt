ROUTABLE/NON-ROUTABLE IP RANGES
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/528538/non-routable-ip-address
For IPV4 the non-routable addresses ranges are from 10.1.1.1 to 10.255.255.254, from 172.16.1.1 to 172.31.255.254, and from 192.168.1.1 to 192.168.255.254. These IP addresses will not be recognized outside of a private network.

WHAT IPS TO USE
VM IP (intnet) - 192.168.11.101
VM IP (bridged/host-only) - 192.168.21.101
KUBERNETES POD IP CIDR  - 172.16.0.0/16  (https://serverfault.com/questions/931061/helm-i-o-timeout-kubernetes)
